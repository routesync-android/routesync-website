Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RouteSync

How does calendar export work?

RouteSync reads your selected calendar, extracts appointment details (title, location, time), and formats them into a CSV file that route planning apps can import.

Which route planning apps are supported?

RouteSync generates standard CSV format that works with Circuit Route Planner, Route4Me, OptimoRoute, and most other route optimization apps.

Is my calendar data secure?

Yes! All processing happens locally on your device. Your calendar data is never uploaded to external servers or shared with third parties.

What languages are available?

RouteSync supports English, German, French, and Italian, making it perfect for delivery professionals across Europe and beyond.

How do I sync my Google Calendar?

RouteSync automatically triggers calendar sync before export to ensure you have the latest appointments. No manual sync required.

Can I export multiple days at once?

Currently, RouteSync exports one day at a time to ensure optimal route planning. You can quickly export additional days as needed.
