RouteSync - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 18, 2024
Last Updated: September 18, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to RouteSync. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("RouteSync" or "the App").

RouteSync is designed to help delivery professionals export their calendar appointments to CSV format for use with route planning applications like Circuit Route Planner.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Calendar Data

RouteSync accesses your device's calendar data to:

  • Read calendar events from your selected calendar
  • Extract appointment details (title, location, date, time)
  • Generate CSV exports for route planning

Important: Calendar data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.

2.2 Device Storage

The App accesses your device's Downloads folder to save exported CSV files. No other files or data on your device are accessed.

2.3 App Preferences

We store the following preferences locally on your device:

  • Selected calendar name and ID
  • Language preference
  • Theme preference (Professional Blue or Giraffe Pink)
  • First-launch status

2.4 Data We Do NOT Collect

RouteSync does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Network or internet activity

3. How We Use Your Information

The information accessed by RouteSync is used solely for:

  • CSV Generation: Converting your calendar appointments into CSV format
  • App Functionality: Displaying calendar selection and export status
  • User Experience: Remembering your language and theme preferences
  • Integration: Launching external route planning applications

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage Only

All data processing occurs locally on your device. RouteSync does not:

  • Upload data to remote servers
  • Store data in cloud services
  • Transmit data over the internet
  • Share data with third-party services

4.2 Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, you maintain full control over your information. The App uses Android's built-in security features to protect data access.

5. Permissions Explained

RouteSync requests the following Android permissions:

5.1 Calendar Access (READ_CALENDAR)

Purpose: To read your calendar events for CSV export

Data Used: Event titles, locations, dates, and times from your selected calendar only

5.2 Sync Settings (WRITE_SYNC_SETTINGS)

Purpose: To ensure calendar data is up-to-date before export

Function: Triggers calendar synchronization with your calendar provider (e.g., Google Calendar)

6. Third-Party Applications

RouteSync can launch external route planning applications (such as Circuit Route Planner) but does not share your calendar data with these applications. The CSV files you generate are separate files that you can choose to import into other applications at your discretion.

7. Data Retention

RouteSync retains data only as long as necessary:

  • Calendar Data: Accessed temporarily during export, not stored permanently
  • App Preferences: Stored until you uninstall the app or clear app data
  • CSV Files: Saved to your Downloads folder under your control

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Calendar Access: Can be revoked at any time through Android settings
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all stored preferences
  • CSV Files: You can delete exported CSV files from your Downloads folder
  • Calendar Selection: You can change or deselect calendars within the app

9. Children's Privacy

RouteSync is intended for professional use by delivery drivers and logistics professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

10. International Users

RouteSync is available in multiple languages (English, German, French, Italian) but does not collect location data or process data differently based on geographic location.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Significant changes will be communicated through app updates
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

12. Google Play Store Compliance

This privacy policy complies with Google Play Store requirements and accurately reflects RouteSync's data practices as declared in the Play Console Data Safety section.
